Some Must-Know Men’s Shirt Styling Features

Solid same colour combo: It is a highly recommended under-used style option. Wearing the same colour pants elongates your body so that you appear taller and thinner. Buy a shirt online from our collection that has shades of blue or neutrals for the best look. You can accessorise this clean casual look with your favourite shoes or a great belt.

Tone-on-tone casual wear: Another great version of solid colour outfits is the tone-on-tone casual outfit. Pick something from full sleeve shirts for men’s and pair it with a bottom that has a slight amount of colour difference. Make sure that the hue matches. You can go for navy pants with a light blue shirt or try some shades of grey for a very modern look.

Colour top with a neutral bottom: Buy shirts in dark colours such as blue, olive, and burgundy and pair them with neutral colour bottoms to flaunt a stylish look.

Colour bottom with a neutral top: You may also choose to buy a shirt online in a neutral shade from our collection and pair it with a matching colour bottom. Remember that if the colours are not coordinated properly, they might give you a busy/messy look instead.

Shine with black: Black goes with everything and that makes it the favourite for every guy. It goes with every skin tone, and body type, you really can’t go wrong with this colour.

Buttons are important: You can get a full-button placket shirt online for formal and festive occasions. The half-button shirts, on the other hand, will be ideal for corporate exclusives.

Hemline matters as well: Hemline in a shirt accentuates its appeal and adds a tinge of style at the bottom.

Semi-casual prints: If you have a corporate event lined up and you need a semiformal touch, buy a men’s shirt with conversational prints or a geometric printed formal shirt to make that perfect impression.
