FAQs on Drop Cut T-Shirts

How to Style Long -Sleeved Drop Cut T-Shirts?

Drop cut long sleeve t-shirts look great with washed jeans and men’s joggers. Finish off with a pair of light-coloured sneakers to get the compliments pouring in.

How to Wash Men’s Drop Cut Long Sleeve T-Shirts?

Be it a short sleeved or drop cut long sleeved t-shirts, the care techniques are the same. Wash in cold water with a mild detergent. Do not bleach, iron directly or tumble dry. Dry in the shade for best results.

Can Men’s Drop Cut Long Sleeve Shirts be Worn in Winters?

Since these are made of 100% cotton, they might not be the best choice for very cold weather. During the winter, pair them with bomber or leather jackets for extra style.

What are the Best Colors in Drop Cut Shirts for Men?

Look for red, sage black, lime green, teal blue, grey mélange & neon and maroon. They can offer undisputed style all through the year.
