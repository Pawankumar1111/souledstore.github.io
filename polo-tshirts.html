Tips on Styling a Polo T-shirt

Plain single-coloured polo t-shirts can be styled with printed shorts to sport a beach look; ideal for those warm days.

On colder days, pair white printed polo t-shirts with light-coloured cardigans or jackets for men. Finish the look with jeans to keep yourself warm and stylish.

Slip in a polo tee with joggers or sweatpants for an ultra-casual look. Upturn the collars and team up with funky slippers for a fun appearance.

To wear tees at your workplace, simply tuck them in your trousers. The best colours could be grey, black, or blue. Pair with boots or loafers and you are good to go.

For a more edgy look, accessorize your neutral polo tee with sunnies and loafers.

FAQs on Polo T-shirts

How to Style Polo T-shirts at a Party?

Pick polo t-shirts online from our vast collection of quirky prints and dual colours. Throw in a leather jacket, canvas espadrilles, and classic watches to look party-ready.

Can You Wear Polo T-shirts with Sneakers?

Yes, polo t-shirts can be paired with sneakers, loafers, or any other kind of shoes for a smart look. You can try both contrasting and matching colours, according to your preference.

Which is the Best Material for Polo T-shirts?

Cotton is the right knit, since it is extremely breathable and offers a simplistic look. It also keeps your body cool during summers. So, choose polo t-shirts made of 100% cotton t-shirts for ease and comfort.
