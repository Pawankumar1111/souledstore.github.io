About Men's Tank Tops and Vests

Sleeveless t-shirts are t-shirts without sleeves. They can be worn by people of any gender and any age. Sleeveless tees have multiple uses - they can be used as undershirts or worn for athletic activities or as home attire in hot/humid weather. Throughout the world, they are known by various names - muscle shirt in America, marcel in France, mayka in Russia, sando in the Philippines, sando-genji or banian in India. No matter what you call them as you will definitely love our tank tops and vests collection.

FAQs

1. What is the difference between tank tops and vests?
Though both tank tops and vests are sleeveless, there is one major difference between them: while vests have broader shoulder straps and narrower armholes, tank tops have narrow shoulder straps and larger armholes.

2. Are vests and tank tops to be worn only in gyms?
Tank tops and vests, though primarily seen as workout apparel, can be worn outside of gyms as well. You can wear them for a jog, a hike, a night in with friends or even even a camping trip.

YES! All our tank tops and vests are made with 100% cotton. We only use premium quality bio-washed cotton to ensure your tank tops/vests are the comfiest part of your wardrobe.
