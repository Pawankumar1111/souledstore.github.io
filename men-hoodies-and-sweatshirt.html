Styling Tips for men’s hoodies and men’s sweatshirts

  • Pair colours nicely. Although hoodies for men and sweatshirts for men are considered informal, and there is a fair amount of liberty to play with colours, be careful. Block colouring can be achieved by matching two contrasting colours together. You can pair a vibrant yellow men’s hoodie with an equally splashy pair of trousers of Royal blue or red. Or you can take a red men’s hoodie and pair it up with classic blue. If it works for Superman, Spiderman, Captain America, and Dr Strange, chances are, it will work for you.

  • The best men’s hoodies will work brilliantly with track pants or sweatpants. Whether you are keeping your motivation up for that early morning leg day at the gym or kicking yourself out of bed for a run, stylish men’s hoodies will get you going in no time. Check out our collection of brand new Joggers for men’s. They are comfy, fun, and go really well with hoodies and sweatshirts alike. Pair the Harry Potter: Hogwarts alumni grey men’s sweatshirt with a jet-black Deathly Hallows Jogger to levitate your Monday mornings.

  • The best hoodies for men are always a little oversized; not to the point where they’re baggy, but comfortable and cosy. Wear a straight cut or regular fit pair of denim. If you want winter wear that looks sharp and form-fitting, give our winter sweatshirts a go. They match excellently with skinny or slim-fit trousers and look dashing & elegant. They go well for a party, get together, or a casual coffee meeting with friends or colleagues at the office.

  • Contrast is the key. You can wear hoodies and sweatshirts without any additional tee or shirt. Don’t be afraid to try light colours in upper wear. A nice pink hoodie (men’s) will go nicely with any pair of jeans and will soften your appearance. With pastels in style, you could experiment with some good-looking hoodies. If you are feeling like a pro, get your hands on a nice white men’s hoodie. It will look casual and airy with a nice pair of slim-fit black jeans.
